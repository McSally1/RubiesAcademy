ABOUT US

We joyfully welcome you on board! Rubies Academy is a fast growing school aimed at nurturing leaders that will positively transform our society. It does this through inculcating principles such as diligence, hard work, confidence, godliness and godliness and respect. Our "Rubies" as they are called are taught using play-way, practical and visual methods thus resulting in their improved performance. Our Vision is to partner with parents to provide quality education so that each Ruby can attain his/her maximium potentials within a caring, secure and friendly environment.
